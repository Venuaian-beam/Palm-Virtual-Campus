Welcome to Palm Virtual Campus !

Palm Virtual Campus is designed to make online learning effective and engaging. We aim to bridge the gap between students and their academic success by providing seamless access to tutors, resources, and virtual classrooms.






Palm Virtual campus provides you the experience of campus life even from the comfort of your homes and hostels.

This platform gives student the avenue to view their courses for the semester, view their SIP, view their account balance at the cafeteria and make orders to be delivered, take notes etc.

We understand that in tertiary education, group discussions are key for teaching and learning therefore PAVCA allows student and lecturers to create groups for students and organize classes for them .

Now the fun part, there is a live chat session for each group created so that students and lecturers can reach out to each other easily and faster even from the comfort of their hostel.
